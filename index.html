Project Description

According to many scholars, the Desertification has different definitions and forms. In this research, we are looking for land degraduation due to sandification or advancement of the Sahara. In order to fully understand the desertification issue, the United Nation Convention to Combat Desertification (UNCCD) suggests the DPSIR concepts (Driving Forces, Pressure, State, Impact and R stands for Responses). In other words, the State variable of the concept will be explored in this research.
